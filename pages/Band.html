Kellin Quinn

Kellin Quinn was born on 24 April 1986 in Medford, Oregon. He is the son of Jeuse Bostwick and Mary, and has two brothers and two sisters. He started showing interest in music when he was in school and began playing the trumpet. Currently, he is married to Katelynn Quinn and has two stepsons, Liam and Rowan, and one daughter named Copeland. He has been part of SWS band since 2009, and has come a long way since his humble beginning. He has been a part of various other bands as well, and is responsible for producing different kinds of music. Besides being the powerful vocalist and singer that he is, Kellin Quinn is also an excellent songwriter and composer. His powerful words have made a deep impact on many people. He is also known to be a remarkable keyboardist, which just makes him all the more sought-after.

Nick Martin

Nick Martin was born on December 10, 1982 (age 38) in San Diego, California, United States. He is a cousin of Vic and Mike Fuentes from the band Pierce The Veil. He got married to Jenna Silva in 2016. Martin began playing guitar when he was 10 years old and was raised on an eclectic spectrum of music by his father. He was greatly inspired by Queen & The Beatles at a young age.

Justin Hills

Justin Hills was born on November 19, 1987, in Houston, Texas. He has a younger sister named Ivory. He's the bassist of SWS. Prior to joining Sleeping with Sirens, he was a member of another group named For All We Know. A native of Texas, he has always aspired to play the guitar. Sleeping with Sirens released their debut album, ‘With Ears to See and Eyes to Hear’, in March 2010. The lead single of that album, ‘If I'm James Dean, You're Audrey Hepburn’, became their first major hit song. Since then, Hills and his group have released four more studio albums, ‘Let’s Cheer to This’, ‘Feel’, ‘Madness’, and ‘Gossip’, one extended play, ‘If You Were a Movie, This Would Be Your Soundtrack’, one live album, ‘Live and Unplugged’, and one box set ‘The Rise Years’. They have embarked on multiple US and world tours, including the 2017 Up Close and Personal Gossip tour. Hills’ popularity as a musician has found its way to social media. On Instagram, he has several hundreds of thousands of followers.

Jack Fowler

Jack was born on January 25, 1992 in Florida. He picked an interest in music since he was little as it had always been a passion for him. Jack was previously a member of the rock band Broadway but he left due to their differences. He joined Kellin Quinn, Justin Hills, and Gabe Barham in SWS in 2011. He got engaged to his long time girlfriend Lauren LeCompte in August 2019.

Matty Best

He's the new drummer of the band. He joined the band in 2019 after Gabe Baraham left, following the release of How it feels to be lost.
